Fusion 360 Fusion Cuisine

Ready to see what this restaurant has to offer? Click below.
At Fusion 360, located in the heart of Lincroft, we believe in offering a world-class culinary experience – one that brings you food from every corner of the Earth, or all “360 degrees” of it.

America is a melting pot of cultures, and you can find a restaurant for just about every culture around the globe in this country. Our establishment tackles the difficult problem of combining these palates and turns it into a beautiful expression of food, multiculturalism, and style. We’re what you might call the melting pot of the food world! We strive to bring out the best in traditional recipes and put a spin on them that is sure to delight the senses, from the spice at the tip of your tongue to the smell of rose suffusing your mind. If the menu isn’t enough to leave you wanting more at each visit, the contemporary, casual vibes at the restaurant will enhance your fine dining experience!
